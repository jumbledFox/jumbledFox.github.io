Music

I've been making little songs in FL Studio for a little while now, they're not the best, but I love 'em!

Just a warning: The names are very iffy, whenever I save a song I put the name as whatever's in my mind.

I made these using lots of different (and probably quite goofy) methods. I use a plugin called Prophanity, as well as the Earthbound soundfont, and others!!

THE LATER YEARS

This stuff is more recent!

Around this time I stopped using Neochip due to it always crashing!!! I switched over to a lovely synthesiser called Prophanity.

Klik.wav

Inspired by popular youtuber Kliksphilip and his father's music, I made this little track. I quite enjoy its repetitive melody, one that was achieved by randomly clicking around on the piano roll.

Ringer.mp3

This was just me goofily messing around, but I like it enough to put it here.

Hyper2.mp3

A nice little melody I made, again, all of these need to be seriously developed before they turn into anything more than a concept. Don't ask for the version with vocals, I wish to forget ever trying to sing...

Cassiooo.mp3

I made this one for a Swag Weasels sequel, its currently in mid-development hell...

Steventest.mp3

Another one inspired by the style of Kliksphilip's father, Steventhedreamer. I took inspiration from his song Electronic Voluntary, although I could never compete with perfection.

Fox.mp3

Wowzers, another one inspired by them.. it's almost like the songs I listen to all of the time have an impact on me! Steventhedreamer's album Synchobonk Collection is a brilliant listen, by the way.

Amelodiuswave.mp3

This one I just love, the ascending notes and crispy warm square waves create a lovely sense of nostalgia, I was inspired to make this when I listened to some of the Undertale soundtrack (Most notably Fallen Down) and felt reminiscent of the past. And before you ask, I KNOW THAT I SPELT IT WRONG!!!
